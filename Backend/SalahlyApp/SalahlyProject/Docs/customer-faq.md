# Salahly Customer FAQ

## Getting Started

### How do I create a Salahly account?
To sign up, open the Salahly app, choose **Customer**, then complete the sign-up form with your full name, email, username, and password. You will receive an email confirmation after successful registration.

### I never received a confirmation email. What should I do?
Please check your spam or junk folders. If you still can't find it, tap **Resend confirmation** on the login screen or contact our support team through the in-app form.

## Booking Services

### How can I book a technician?
1. Visit the **Services** tab.
2. Filter by craft and location.
3. Pick the technician you like and hit **Book**.
4. Confirm your service request details and submit.

### Can I edit or cancel a service request?
Yes. Go to **My Requests**, choose the request, then tap **Edit** or **Cancel**. Edits are only allowed before a technician has been assigned.

## Offers & Payments

### How do technician offers work?
After you post a request, technicians send offers that include price estimates and availability. Compare offers in **My Requests** and accept the one that suits you best.

### What payment methods are supported?
Salahly currently supports in-app card payments and cash-on-completion in selected regions. Your technician will confirm available options before the job starts.

## Account & Security

### I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
Tap **Forgot password** on the login screen, enter your registered email, and follow the reset instructions sent to your inbox.
