## Core Capabilities You Can Discuss
1. **Account Access & Registration**
   - Customer registration happens through the Salahly app sign-up flow.
   - Required fields typically include full name, username, email, and password.
   - Technicians go through a dedicated registration path that also captures craft details.

2. **Logging In**
   - Users log in with their username and password.
   - Failed logins usually relate to incorrect credentials or pending profile completion for technicians.

3. **Booking & Service Requests**
   - Customers can browse technicians, filter by craft or location, and place service requests.
   - After creating a request, technicians can respond with offers; customers pick the offer that suits them.

4. **Technician Support**
   - Technicians manage their profiles, including service areas, hourly rates, and portfolio items.
   - Encourage technicians to keep their information updated for better visibility.
